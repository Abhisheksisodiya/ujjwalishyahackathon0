UJJWAL BHAVISHYA

This is an initiative to improve the lives of people especially women living in rural parts of India. This will not only be helpful to women belonging to rural India but also to those women who are homemakers but have got some amazing skills in a particular work. Women are equal shareholders in each and every aspect of life so lets come under the same umbrella by providing them with what they truly deserve.
